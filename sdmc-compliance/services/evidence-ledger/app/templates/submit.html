🏛️ How this works
Your submission is evaluated against SDMC compliance rules by Open Policy Agent (OPA) running Rego policies in real-time. On approval, a SHA-256 evidence record is cryptographically chained to the immutable audit ledger. Client-provided hashes are always discarded — the server recomputes them. Per 21 CFR Part 11 §11.10(e) and NIST SP 800-53 AU-9.
